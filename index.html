How it works

Wealth Management

Step 1

Risk Profiling

A process to help determine the optimal levels of investment risk for clients.

Step 2

Reviewing Assets & Liabilities

Points out use of book values versus market values, which can under or over inflate.

Step 3

Primary & Secondary Goals

Considering goals like Child Planning, Education, Marriage, Home purchase.

Step 4

Need and Want Analysis

Brainstorm about product features and services you would like to see.

Step 5

Considering Standard of Living

Consider level of comfort, material goods, and necessities available to you.

Step 6

Detailed Financial Plan

Provide current money situation & strategies to achieve long-term goals.

Step 7

Investment Products

Planning stocks, bonds, options, derivatives and other financial instruments.

Step 8

Allocate asset & construct portfolio

Aims to balance risk and reward by apportioning a portfolio's assets.

Step 9

Retirement Planning

Allocation of savings or revenue for retirement to achieve independence.

Step 10

Tax and Estate Planning

Bequest of assets to heirs and the settlement of estate taxes.
